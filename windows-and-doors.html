Windows & Doors, Your Way

Timber. Aluminium. Thermally Broken. One Studio, Infinite Possibilities.

At Durabal Studio, we offer a versatile selection of window and door systems tailored to meet the demands of both design and performance. Whether you’re drawn to the organic warmth of timber, the sleek durability of aluminium, or the energy efficiency of thermally broken frames, our range ensures your vision is never limited by material.

Each system comes with its own strengths: timber delivers exceptional insulation and natural character, standard aluminium is lightweight and low-maintenance, while thermally broken aluminium significantly reduces heat transfer—achieving low U-values ideal for energy-conscious homes. With expert guidance at every step, we help you choose the material that perfectly balances style, comfort, and long-term value.

Strength & Versatility

Engineered Timber

Combining natural beauty with enhanced durability, engineered timber offers a sustainable choice with superior thermal performance.

Classic & Elegant

Solid Timber

Known for its timeless appeal and warmth, solid timber requires maintenance but rewards with unmatched aesthetic charm.

Modern & Durable

Aluminium

The preferred choice for sleek, minimal designs, aluminium is low-maintenance and offers excellent strength with reduced visible metal.

Robust & Industrial

Steel

While strong, steel is often outshone by aluminium due to its tendency to corrode and lack of thermal advantages.

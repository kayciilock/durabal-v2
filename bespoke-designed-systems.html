The Process

Brief → Design → Tooling & Samples → Testing & Certification

  1. 01

    Brief

    Define constraints, interfaces, loads, lifecycle, finishes, and compliance targets.

  2. 02

    Design

    First-principles modelling, DFM details, tolerance stacks, and motion profiling.

  3. 03

    Tooling & Samples

    Rapid tooling, pilot runs, and pre-production samples for fit/function sign-off.

  4. 04

    Testing & Certification

    AS/NZS and project-specific protocols—structural, air/water, durability, acoustics.

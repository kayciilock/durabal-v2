Choosing the Right Glazing: A Guide to Optimal Performance

Choosing the right glazing maximises energy efficiency and aesthetic appeal by optimising thermal performance to suit heating or cooling needs.

Reeded

Known for its decorative texture, reeded glass provides privacy without completely obstructing light.

Frosted

Ideal for privacy, frosted glass diffuses light, creating a soft glow while obscuring visibility.

Clear/Low-E

This glazing allows most visible light through while reflecting a significant portion of infrared light to enhance energy efficiency.

Certified Excellence: Meeting the Highest Standards

Ensure your building projects comply with the most stringent performance criteria by selecting glazing systems that meet AS2047, NatHERS, and Section J standards.

AS2047

This certification guarantees that windows and doors meet Australian standards for performance, covering structural integrity, water penetration, and air infiltration.

NatHERS

The Nationwide House Energy Rating Scheme rates the energy efficiency of residential buildings, helping you achieve optimal thermal comfort.

Section J

This part of the National Construction Code focuses on energy efficiency requirements for commercial buildings, ensuring systems meet sustainability goals.

Pivot

Pivot doors are a striking architectural statement, rotating on a central axis to create a dramatic entryway. They offer a seamless transition between spaces and are ideal for large, heavy doors where a traditional hinge might not suffice.

Hinged

The classic choice for many homes, hinged doors swing open on hinges, typically mounted to the side of the door frame. They are versatile and suitable for both interior and exterior use, providing a straightforward and reliable motion.

French

Known for their elegance, French doors consist of two hinged doors that open outward or inward from the centre. They often feature glass panels, allowing for natural light while offering a sophisticated aesthetic appeal.

Sliding

Sliding doors glide along a track, saving space and offering a modern look. They are perfect for areas where a swinging door might be impractical, such as closets or patio entries, providing a sleek and unobtrusive solution.

Bifolding

These doors fold back in sections, creating a concertina effect that opens up entire walls to the outdoors or between rooms. They are excellent for maximising space and creating a seamless indoor-outdoor experience.

Lift & Slide

Combining the best of sliding doors with enhanced sealing, lift & slide doors lift off their tracks to slide open smoothly, offering superior energy efficiency and ease of use. They are ideal for large glass panels, providing unobstructed views and access.

Slide & Fold

Similar to bifolding doors, slide & fold doors stack neatly to one side, enabling a wide opening. They are perfect for connecting indoor and outdoor spaces, allowing for flexible use of space and abundant natural light.

At the heart of our home automation offering is VOLTA , a powered sliding door system that redefines ease and elegance. Inspired by the leap from manual to power steering in vehicles, VOLTA gives you fingertip control over even the heaviest door panels, seamlessly integrating into your existing frames. The motor is hidden. The tech is quiet. The control is intuitive. Whether you’re moving a single panel or a multi-panel sliding system, VOLTA makes every motion effortless , with optional tram-style powered rails and full app or voice control for next-level convenience.
